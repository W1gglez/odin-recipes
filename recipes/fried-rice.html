Fried Rice

Description

This copycat fried rice recipe tastes just like your favorite restaurant but even better! Loaded with tons of vegetables, scrambled eggs, leftover white rice and my secret ingredient: Butter!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter, divided
  • 3 eggs, whisked
  • 2 medium carrots, small dice
  • 1 small onion, small dice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peasa, thawed
  • 4 cups day old rice
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • sliced green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of butter into a large skillet. Add the eggs and scramble until fully cooked. Remove them from the pan and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining tablespoon of butter into the pan. Add carrots and onion to the pan and cook until tender, 3-4 minutes. Stir in garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Add in the cold rice and peas and sauté for 3-4 minutes. The rice should brown up a bit.
  4. Add in the cold rice and peas and sauté for 3-4 minutes. The rice should brown up a bit.
